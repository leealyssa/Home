Limiting Unhealthy Foods and Eating Healthy Meals

Some people underestimate the importance of how eating certain types of foods play a crucial role in your everyday life. The first rule to know when starting your fitness journey is, make sure you eat breakfast everyday and have healthy nutritious meals. Rather than choosing foods with fat, sugar, and calories, pick foods with more protein and fibre. You don’t need additional food to make up for mild or moderate quantities of physical activity. Listen to your body to figure out how much food you should consume.

Certain foods and drinks are advertised to help increase your sports performance such as:

  • Energy Gels
  • Sports Drinks
  • Protein Powders
  • Performance Bars

These foods and drinks have significant sodium, sugar, and saturated fat content. Choose to eat nutritious meals that make you feel energized. Keep in mind, healthy meals do not always have to be bland! There are many healthy recipes online which you can follow to make your eating enjoyable. For example, Eatingwell.com has 20 healthy meals you can cook in 20 minutes!. If you have a tight schedule, these recipes are non-time consuming, healthy, and tasty!
